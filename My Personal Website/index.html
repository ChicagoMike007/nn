This is a small story about how Michael Wendes received his nickname - ChicagoMike

Back in the year 2002, while Michael was in Birmingham Alabama working at the county election warehouse testing the newest method saving the votes to the reporting. Well this manager name Joe comes strolling in from the home office in Chicago, Illinios, says hi to several people then looks at me and says "Hey Chicago Mike" I looked around thinking he was talking to someone else, but nope. He said that was my new nickname and he was going to call me that to help distingwish me from the three other Mikes or Michaels he knew in from our company (the home office was and still is in Omaha, Nebraska.
